Thunder™

THUNDER is a captivating and exhilarating game experience, allowing you to blend hero creation and storytelling lore with theatrical, movie and stage concert elements to build up the dramatic narrative.

Balancing the best of FPS and RPG MMO gameplay. THUNDER is social — share it with your friends and meet online to play together, leveling up as you gain experience. THUNDER is competitive — you are challenged to think fast and play hard in a live, team-vs.-team game. We are developing a marketplace App that works at the heart of our game to offer you virtual Goods that only increase in value and rarity!
