Thunder™

THUNDER is a team vs. team multiplayer, high-fantasy, first-person sci-fi shooter where players aim high-tech devices. These highly proficient tools get multiple jobs done. Extremely powerful to begin with, however if you customize the device recoil (venting energy) too high, it will propel you sailing through the air; that’s the way we like them set! (Think the ultimate rocket jump).

Now… that’s just the justice of our right hands! Our Left hands holds our royal scepters to call lightning and bring down THUNDEROUS shockwaves of lightning, fire, ice and storms upon our enemies. We live for this thrill. This rush is our arena, THUNDER is our sport, we play online and the stakes are high! THUNDER is more than a "thrilling game” in the way you move, attack and retreat, just like a 'rollercoaster ride - off the rails’. It is a world that frees people and enriches them into our future of gameplay wealth. THUNDER is more than a game; it is a world that frees people and enriches them in the gameplay of the future. THUNDER is a game that ends the "noob to slaughter", "camper", "sniper", “death” count outs, and “hacker” problems often found in other games. We have designed a game environment where these weeds cannot grow.
